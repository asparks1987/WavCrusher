# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project where people can discuss preservation, software, audio, and mistakes respectfully. Technical rigor and kindness are compatible.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- Make room for newcomers and domain experts alike.
- Disclose uncertainty and correct errors openly.
- Protect private data and unpublished recordings.
- Treat data-loss reports with urgency and care.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption, or sharing private material without permission are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Before a public community launch, maintainers must publish a real confidential conduct-reporting route and an enforcement procedure. Until then, this document states project expectations but does not pretend a staffed escalation channel exists.
